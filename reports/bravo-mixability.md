## 9. Pack Identity

**Bravo Pack** is a **120 BPM warm/energetic pack** with:
- Strong percussive beats (kick, taiko, hat layers)
- Clean bass lines (dry pick, pop, sub wobble)
- Glassy + guitar melodies (bright synth over relaxed guitar)
- Atmospheric space textures
- A minor female vocals with F major complement
- Rhythmic FX clicker + sine loop as performer layers
- Short one-shot FX (scratch, sub drop) for dramatic moments

It fills the **"upbeat organic + electronic hybrid"** niche — different from Cyberpunk (dark/electric), Core Mix (minimal house), and New Pack Alpha (cinematic/trap).
